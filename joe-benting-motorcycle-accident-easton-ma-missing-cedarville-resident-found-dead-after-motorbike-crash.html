Joe Benting Obituary Easton, MA: Missing Cedarville resident found dead after motorbike crash.

23-year-old Joe Benting of Cedarville died tragically as a result of injuries sustained in a road traffic collision involving a motorcycle, an online obituary announced on Tuesday, October 24, 2023.

Joe had earlier been report missing by his family via social media posts.

“I need help my son Joe Benting (23yo) is missing! Last seen Sunday night 10/22 @10:30pm leaving a bar in Easton on his white street bike heading home to Cedarville. I think joggers. He was wearing plaid hoodie. Helmet white with gold skulls on it and the American flag face cover. He had his grey backpack. If you have seen him please message me!,” a post shared on Plymouth Connection – Massachusetts on Facebook reported Joe missing.

The details concerning the circumstances surrounding the crash are yet to be released, as investigations continue.
